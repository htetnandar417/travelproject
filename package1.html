Yangon Mandalay Kyaing Tong

Your Journey Day By Day

Day 1 Arrival Yangon

Arrive at Yangon International Airport in the morning. Meet with your guide and then transfer to your hotel for check-in (if early check-in is available). After refreshment at hotel then transfer to local restaurant for lunch.

Day 2 Yangon – Mandalay

After breakfast at hotel, transfer to Yangon airport for your domestic flight from Yangon to Mandalay. From the airport, drive to Amarapura and visit U-Bein Bridge.

Day 3 Mandalay – Kyaing Tong

Pickup from your hotel and transfer to Mandalay airport for your domestic flight from Mandalay to Kyaing Tong. Arrive at Kyaing Tong Airport in the afternoon and transfer to your hotel.

Day 4 Kyaing Tong and Villages

Enjoy breakfast at hotel. This morning drive out of Kyaing Tong to Kattauk Village and then to Pin Tauk Village where you can see Akha and Lahu people.

Day 5 Kyaing Tong and Villages

Enjoy breakfast at hotel. Drive out of Kyaing Tong and visit to Hokyin Mountain. Visit around the tribal villages and enjoy a packed lunch on the way.

Day 6 Kyaing Tong – Yangon

Enjoy breakfast at hotel. This morning, visit Kyaing Tong market others interesting places and lunch at local restaurant.

Day 7 Yangon – Departure

Breakfast at hotel. This morning, transfer to Yangon Airport for your departure flight…
